Who We Are

The VisionBlue is a non-profit, non-governmental organization founded in 2018 to empower young women and girls in Cameroon, especially those between the ages of 15 and 25 who face poverty, displacement, lack of education, and social exclusion.

What We Do

  • Professional and vocational training
  • Business start-up grants
  • Mentoring and supervision
  • Health and hygiene education
  • Psychological and social support
  • Scholarships and school assistance

About Us

The VisionBlue was founded in January 2018 by Ms. Maryanne M. Gwaabe and a group of young professionals from Cameroon and the diaspora. Our goal is to give young women the tools, confidence, and support they need to become independent and live with dignity.

Our Programs

Skills & Training

We support girls to acquire professional skills in IT, fashion, beauty, trade, and other fields.

Business Grants

We provide carefully monitored, partially refundable grants to help young women start small businesses or continue education.

Mentoring & Follow-up

Each beneficiary is assigned a mentor to ensure accountability and success.

Health & Hygiene

We organize health talks, hygiene education, and psychological support for girls and young mothers.

Our Impact Model

Our “Aid to Aid” model ensures sustainability: once established, beneficiaries help others through refunds, mentoring, and knowledge sharing.
